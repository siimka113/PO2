About Macaron

Company: Macaron

A small Montreal-based patisserie, well known for making light, airy macarons and their cute mascot (a mouse named Ronnie)

The company targets customers of all ages, but especially those with an affinity for cute things and good desserts, individuals who post their food on social media with carefully crafted captions.

Macaron entices potential consumers by offering a seamless ordering experience, with consistent visual design to promote the brandâ€™s reliability - if the website looks so good, imagine how good the macarons taste...

Our website focuses on minimalist layout and simple colour scheme for readability, with hand drawn graphics to better represent the brand's friendly nature and carefully hand crafted macarons.

Branding Components

Font

Our font pairing consists of Poppins and Single Day. Poppins is known for its versatility and accessibility in various contexts. Its rounder yet highly-structured glyphs align with both the brand aesthetic while providing a great contrast for Single Day, a looser, handwritten font.

Color

We used bright pink as the main accent, as it creates a sense of youthful vitality associated with the brand. For our darkest colour (in place of black), we used a rich velvet brown to create a subtle association with cocoa. Together, the two colours compliment each other nicely, being analogous on the colour wheel and having a significant difference in contrast.
